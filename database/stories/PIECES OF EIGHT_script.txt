Pieces of Eight

EXT. FLORIDA KEYS - DAY
Thick cumulous clouds fill the warm tropical sky, casting
an eerie darkness over a luxurious 84 foot multi-million
dollar YACHT named TEA PARTY.
Slowly cruising across the calm water.
INT. BRIDGE - DAY
The hired CAPTAIN (50’s), stands proudly at the helm like
a pro. The GPS doing the rest.
His FIRST MATE (20’s), sits in a plush chair listening to
Big Band music. Staring out the smoked-glass windscreen
in front of them.
STATE ROOM
More like a small apartment. Its nicely dressed owners,
HUSBAND and WIFE, both 64 and still athletic, lounge on
soft velvet couches watching live dog races on a flat
screen TV.
WIFE
I told you that would happen.
Never bet on the favorite.
HUSBAND
He ran great at the Nationals.
WIFE
That was last month, honey--
A look of horror crosses her botox infused face.
Causing her Husband to turn toward their WINDOW and see a
large BLACK VESSEL moving alongside them.
Dangerously close.
HUSBAND
Hey, hey! What the hell are they
doing!
Leaping from the couch.

EXT. YACHT - DAY
A more modest, but still million dollar class, 56 foot
sleek and powerful CRAFT named THE BONES in GOLD LETTERS,
moves even closer alongside TEA PARTY.
Three mid 20’s ratty looking beach bums, FELIX SANCHEZ,
JESSE NORTON and MIGUEL CRUZ, jump onboard.
These experienced badass thugs are wearing expensive
RADIO HEADSETS to communicate.
BRIDGE

Pressing a silver .38 Caliber HANDGUN into the Captain’s
chest.
JESSE
Don’t--
Making them both raise their hands in surrender, not
about to die for someone else’s Yacht.
Jesse shuts down the MOTOR. Keying his mic.
JESSE
Bridge’s secure.
And shoves his two PRISONERS out onto the DECK.
STATE ROOM
BANG! Felix and Miguel kick open the door. Each holding
9mm GLOCKS. The poor Husband and Wife SCREAM, helplessly
cowering in a corner.
FELIX
(into headset)
Got two!
Allowing Miguel to take off. Searching the rest of the
vessel.

Grabbing two long TUBES OF THICK PAPER. Running back

Now showing the name ROYAL HONEYMOON.
Then uses the other ROLL to change the REGISTRATION
NUMBERS on the side.
LION FISH
Let’s go. C’mon.
Checking his WATCH.
Their PRISONERS reluctantly crawl across the narrow

Shoving them toward the DRUMS.
HUSBAND

hurt us. Please--
WIFE

YOUNG WOMAN
Mom!

SHOOTS EACH ONE IN THE BACK OF THE HEAD.

help Jesse cut the ROPES. Separating the two YACHTS.

light rain begins to fall.
