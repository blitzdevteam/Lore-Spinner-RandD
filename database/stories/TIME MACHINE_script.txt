INT. CONTROL BOOTH- NIGHT
Small high-tech box with only a few computer lined DESKS.
And four nervous TECHNICIANS standing in front of a thick
WINDOW overlooking a SPACE POD.
Except the POD isn’t on a launch platform.
It sits at the entrance to a TEN FOOT DIAMETER SPINNING
TUBE STRETCHING A MILE LONG. Fully enclosed in a FACTORY
SIZE BUILDING.
With thousands of CABLES running to a massive City Grade
POWER PLANT.
Years of painstaking work. Billions of dollars invested.
All coming together for this one moment. This one POD.
A round shaped PROJECTILE like you would find at NASA or
Space X.
The massive TUBE around it GAINING SPEED SPINNING FASTER.
Inside the POD--
Two civilian ASTRONAUTS strapped into METAL SEATS. Their
tense face’s visible through their HELMETS. Unsure of
what’s about to happen.
One MAN in particular. In the CONTROL BOOTH. His back to
us. Standing away from the others. Watching the
SPEEDOMETER climb by factors of 50.
The apparatus SPINNING even faster. Finally reaching a
target speed creating a BRIGHT SWIRLING ENERGY FIELD
INSIDE THE LENGTH OF THE TUBE.
All four TECHNICIANS smiling. Buzzing with excitement.
Looking back at the FIGURE as if to say ALMOST THERE.
Watching the ENERGY FIELD grow in size-- Intensity.
But also putting too much stress on the TUBE. Surpassing
its limit. Unable to handle the INTENSE HEAT and searing
FRICTION. Overrun with UNSTABLE ENERGY wrapping the POD
IN HUNDREDS OF ELECTRIC TENTACLES.
Causing one of the now SCREAMING ASTRONAUTS to rapidly
MELT. Becoming LIQUID pooling on the floor.
The lone ASTRONAUT tries to free himself but quickly
breaks down into MOLECULAR PARTICLES. FUSING HIS BODY TO
THE SEAT. Becoming one with the METAL.
Pieces of the POD BEGIN BREAKING APART. FIRE RAGING
throughout the SPINNING TUBE.

Followed by an EXPLOSION-- Destroying the entire POD and
half the CONTROL BOOTH. Killing the FOUR TECHNICIANS in a
shower of GLASS, FIRE AND DEBRIS.
Miraculously sparing the unknown FIGURE.
