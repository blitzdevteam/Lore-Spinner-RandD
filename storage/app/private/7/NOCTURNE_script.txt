NOCTURNE
After a public scandal shatters her life, a disgraced Japanese
heiress hires a secretive organization called the Night Movers to
help her disappear—only to discover she was never meant to survive
the process, and must unravel a conspiracy of erotic control,
corporate erasure, and manufactured shame before she’s permanently
rewritten.
Written by
Thomas Wittmer
