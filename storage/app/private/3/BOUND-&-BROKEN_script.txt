BOUND & BROKEN
When three siblings vanish into a sentient book discovered in a
mysterious carnival tent, they’re transported into mythic realms
shaped by memory, fear, and forgotten tales. As they journey
through collapsing worlds, resist the roles written for them, and
uncover the truth of an ancient entity known as the Storywright,
they must fight to reclaim their identities—and write their own
way home.
Written by
Thomas Wittmer
